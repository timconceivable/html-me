art

Tim has been drawing all his life. He has done comics and caricatures, but mostly just doodles for fun. He prefers to draw people, cartoon / comic characters, strange creatures and abstract things. Tim uses Photoshop and Krita for drawing, painting and image manipulation. He uses Inkscape for vector art. For pixels, Tim uses Pyxel Edit, Photoshop, and sometimes Game-Maker Studio's sprite editor. From time to time, Tim takes photos and does photo manipulation.
